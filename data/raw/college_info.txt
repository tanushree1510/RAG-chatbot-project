Founder: Dr. G.T. Raju established BGS College of Engineering and Technology in 2022 under the BGSKH Education Trust, affiliated with Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (SAST).

College History: BGSCET was founded with the vision to provide quality technical education and to contribute to the development of the nation by producing competent engineers.

Campus Location: The college is situated adjacent to Mahalakshmi Metro Station, Mahalakshmipuram, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560086.

Principal: Dr. G K Ravikumar, PhD in Computer Science, with 20+ years of experience in academia.
He has been leading BGS College of Engineering and Technology since 2022, focusing on research-driven education.
